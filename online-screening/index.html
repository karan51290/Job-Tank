We recognize that online screening is a tough process, but we believe that it is an essential tool for employers, educational institutions, healthcare providers, and other organizations to gather relevant information before making important decisions. Our online screening services are designed to be administered remotely and at scale, making it more efficient and cost-effective than traditional screening methods.

Moreover, our online screening services help to reduce bias and increase objectivity in the screening process by removing the influence of factors such as appearance, race, and gender. However, we are aware of the potential drawbacks of online screening, such as the risk of cheating, the possibility of technical difficulties, and the potential for inaccuracy if the screening tools are not well-designed or properly validated.

At Job Tank, we take online screening seriously, and we ensure that our tools are well-designed and properly validated to provide reliable and accurate results. Our online screening services are used carefully and thoughtfully to ensure that they are both reliable and fair.
